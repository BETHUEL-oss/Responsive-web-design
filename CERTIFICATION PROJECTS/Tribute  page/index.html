Albert Einstein

Theoretical physicist, philosopher of science

Albert Einstein and Robert Oppenheimer photographed by Alfred Eisenstaedt, 1947.

Here is a timeline of Albert Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Germany, the spark of a genius begins
  • 1905 - Annus Mirabilis - Publishes four revolutionary papers: photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity, and E=mc2. Physics changes forever.
  • 1915 - Completes General Theory of Relativity - gravity is the curvature of spacetime.
  • 1919 - Solar eclipse observations confirm general relativity. Einstein becomes a global icon overnight.
  • 1921 - Awarded Nobel prize in Physics for the photoelectric effect - foundational to quantam mechanics.
  • 1933 - Flees Germany; settles in the U.S. at Princeton. Never returns to Europe.
  • 1939 - Signs letter to president Roosevelt warning of atomic bombs - helps launch the Manhattan project.
  • 1940 - Becomes a U.S. citizen(retains Swiss citizenship).
  • 1952 - Offered presidency of Israel - humbly declines.
  • 1955 - Dies April 18 in Princetown.Refuses brain removal for study - but it happens anyways. His ideas live on.
Legacy in one line:

From E=mc2 to GPS in your phone - Einstein didn't just see the universe; he rewrote the rules.
